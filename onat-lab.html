Onat Lab

Principal Investigator

Research Focus

Epilepsy & Epileptogenesis

Investigating the molecular and circuit-level mechanisms underlying the transition from a normal to a seizure-prone brain, using the GAERS genetic model of absence epilepsy. Our work spans from gene expression profiling to pharmacological intervention studies.

Neurodevelopmental Diseases

Studying genetic and molecular factors that contribute to neurodevelopmental disorders, with a focus on genetically engineered experimental models. This includes understanding how early brain development influences disease susceptibility.

Translational Neuropharmacology

Bridging basic neuroscience research with potential therapeutic applications. Our lab identifies novel drug targets through transcriptomic analysis and validates them using pharmacological approaches in preclinical models.

GEMSTONE Project

European Commission Horizon Europe Program

Genetically Engineering Experimental Models

GEMSTONE — "Genetically Engineering Experimental Models: Enhancement of Scientific and Technological Excellence and Innovation Potential to Study Neurodevelopmental Diseases"

Led by Acıbadem Mehmet Ali Aydınlar University, GEMSTONE advances scientific and technological capacity for studying neurodevelopmental diseases — particularly Parkinson's disease and epilepsy — through genetically engineered experimental models and collaboration with leading European institutions.

Objectives

  • Advance gene engineering technologies and neuroscience research capabilities
  • Enable bilateral exchange of scientific knowledge with high-ranking European institutions
  • Investigate neurodevelopment and chemogenetic modulation of cortical layer 6b in absence epilepsy
  • Explore alpha-synuclein distribution in conditional SNCA transgenic mice
  • Transfer knowledge and skills for in-house genetically engineered animal model production
